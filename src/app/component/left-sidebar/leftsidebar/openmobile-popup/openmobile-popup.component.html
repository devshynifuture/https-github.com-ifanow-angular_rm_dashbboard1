For better experience download the "My Planner 2.0" mobile app

My Planner app allows you to track your portfolio across asset classes like Mutual Funds, Stocks, Insurance, Fixed Deposits, Post Office Schemes, etc.

Your portfolios are available at a family level with drill-down options available at the family member level.

There are multi-level drill-down options available for a more granular control understanding of your entire financial portfolio..
