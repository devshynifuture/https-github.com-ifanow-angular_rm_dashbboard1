What is Email integration all about? Why should I do it?

Email is an integral part of an advisor’s practice. A lot of critical client interactions happen over email. Sometimes, you may want to checkout some past email exchanges with the client. With the email syncing service, you can view all these emails in one place without having to leave your IFANOW portal.

Moreover, if you read SEBIs regulations, you will see that Email communication is an integral aspect of the compliance that SEBI expects an advisor to adhere to.

How does Email integration work?

We’ve used Gmail’s APIs to integrate your Gmail inbox with IFANOW. You just need to connect your Email account and within minutes you will see your emails will start showing up here in a secure manner. Once done, you can compose new emails, reply to emails and do most of the critical activities that you would otherwise do on Gmail.

How secure is my email data?

We’ve given a lot of thought to the security aspects while creating the tech architecture. To that end, we’ve ensured that NONE of your email data is stored on our servers. It shows up only when you visit the Email tab. Once you logout, the data is erased. We do not have any access to your email password as well.

Do you store my email data on your servers?

No.

Can I unlink my email integration?

Yes. You can do it at any point.

What happens when I unlink my email integration?

When you unlink, all the emails will stop syncing and showing up. Since the email data is not stored on our servers there is no further scope for anything to be done at our end.
