Key parameters

Saving type
Group all years together

This assumption is applicable for 2 multi year goals viz. Education & Vacation.

When such goals have a longer tenure and start immediately, then the client may not be able to save for all goal years together.

In such cases, you can select “Save for individual years”. As each goal year passes by, system calculates requirement for the next applicable goal year. This will show a lower savings required.

Savings status
Break up

This assumption is applicable for all goal types.

Additionally, wherever the retirement period is used (viz. incomes and expenses) the assumptions are picked from here, unless a retirement goal has been added with a different age.

Retirement age
85 Years

This assumption is applicable for retirement goal mainly.

Additionally, wherever the lifetime period is used (viz. incomes and expenses) the assumptions are picked from here, unless a retirement goal has been added with a different life expectancy.

Life expectancy
85 Years

This assumption is applicable for retirement goal mainly.

Additionally, wherever the lifetime period is used (viz. incomes and expenses) the assumptions are picked from here, unless a retirement goal has been added with a different life expectancy.

Insurance planning - Asset growth rate
8%

This assumption is mainly applicable for needs analysis in life insurance planning section.

This is the rate used to calculcate the real rate of return for the dependant’s needs.
