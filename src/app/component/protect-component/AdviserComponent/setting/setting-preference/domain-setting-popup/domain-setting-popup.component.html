Domain setting

What is this option all about?

With this, you get an option to customize the link with which IFANOW is accessed by you, your team and your clients. You have an option to either Partially or Completely white label this link.

What is Partial white label?

Partial white label is where you customize just the subdomain while the main domain remains my-planner.in. E.g. if your company name is ABC Advisors and you wish to have abcadvisors as the sub domain, then the applicable link that you or your clients can access will be https://abcadvisors.my-planner.in.

What is Complete white label?

Complete white label is available if you have a domain of your own. E.g. let's say you own a domain called abcadvisors.in. Then in this case, you can completely white label the link. So, for instance, you can have a completely white labeled link called clients.abcadvisors.in or login.abcadvisors.in

For Complete white label, can I simply link the domain or creating a subdomain is necessary?

A domain can also be linked. But if you have some content already built on it then it is not possible. E.g. lets say that your site www.abcadvisors.in is already your website with some content on it. In such a case, you cannot link the domain.

What happens if Partial as well as Complete white label option is activated?

In this case, preference is given to Complete white label.
